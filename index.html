Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your pest control service.

How long does a pest control treatment take?

Most residential treatments take between 45 minutes and 2 hours, depending on the type and severity of the infestation. Larger commercial properties may require additional time.

Are your treatments safe for children and pets?

Yes. We use professional, registered pest control products and provide clear aftercare instructions to ensure the safety of your family and pets.

Do you offer commercial pest control?

Absolutely. We provide professional pest management solutions for offices, warehouses, retail stores, factories, apartment complexes and healthcare facilities.

What pests do you treat?

We treat cockroaches, ants, rodents, termites, spiders, bed bugs, fleas, flies, mosquitoes, wasps, silverfish and many other common household and commercial pests.

Do you provide free quotations?

Yes. We offer free, no-obligation quotations. Simply call us, send us a WhatsApp message or complete the enquiry form and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

How quickly can you come out?

We aim to respond as quickly as possible and offer same-day service where availability allows.

Will one treatment solve the problem?

Many infestations are resolved after one treatment, while more severe infestations may require follow-up visits depending on the pest involved.

Which areas do you service?

We service Randburg and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm availability in your location.
